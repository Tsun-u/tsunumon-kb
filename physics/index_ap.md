## Important Note on Course Structure

As of the 2024-25 school year, AP Physics 1 has been reorganized into **8 units** (previously 7).
Key changes:
- Circular motion moved into Unit 2 (Force and Translational Dynamics)
- Rotational motion split into Unit 5 (Torque and Rotational Dynamics) and Unit 6 (Energy and Momentum of Rotating Systems)
- Simple Harmonic Motion is now Unit 7 (Oscillations)
- **Unit 8 (Fluids) is entirely new**

## Core Skill Areas

Six interconnected skill areas are developed throughout the course:

1. **Representations** -- Building and interpreting physical models, diagrams, and graphs
2. **Quantitative Reasoning** -- Applying mathematical tools to set up, solve, and verify physics problems
3. **Inquiry** -- Formulating testable questions and evaluating scientific claims
4. **Lab Design** -- Planning experiments and selecting appropriate measurement strategies
5. **Data Interpretation** -- Processing measurements to identify patterns and support conclusions
6. **Scientific Reasoning** -- Constructing, communicating, and critiquing physical explanations

### Free Response Question Types
- **QQT** (Qualitative/Quantitative Translation): 1 question, 25 min
- **Short Answer**: 2 questions, 18 min each
- **Paragraph Argument Short Answer**: 1 question, 25 min
