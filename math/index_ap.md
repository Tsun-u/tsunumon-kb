# AP® Calculus AB & BC Curriculum Reference

> AP Calculus AB and BC content overview, organized by the College Board course framework.
> Three organizing concepts: **Change (CHA)**, **Limits (LIM)**, **Analysis of Functions (FUN)**
> Last updated: 2026-06-10
>
> **Upcoming changes:** Minor clarifications to the course framework are expected in summer 2026 for the 2026-27 school year — course content itself does not change. Starting with the May 2027 exams, the number of multiple-choice questions and section timing will change (see current College Board documentation). The unit structure and exam weightings below remain current.

## Mathematical Practices

1. **Implementing Mathematical Processes** — Applying mathematical procedures and rules to find values and expressions
2. **Connecting Representations** — Moving fluently across graphical, numerical, algebraic, and verbal representations
3. **Justification** — Constructing clear reasoning and supporting mathematical conclusions
4. **Communication and Notation** — Writing mathematics accurately using standard notation and mathematical conventions

|------|-------|---------------|---------------|

## BC-Only Topics (not tested on AB exam)
- **6.11** Integration by Parts
- **6.12** Integration Using Linear Partial Fractions
- **6.13** Evaluating Improper Integrals
- **7.5** Euler's Method
- **7.9** Logistic Models with Differential Equations
- **8.13** Arc Length of a Smooth, Planar Curve and Distance Traveled
- **Unit 9** (all topics) — Parametric, Polar, Vector-Valued Functions
- **Unit 10** (all topics) — Infinite Sequences and Series

## Exam Format (AB and BC identical structure)

| Section | Part | Questions | Time | Calculator |
|---------|------|-----------|------|------------|
| I (MCQ) — 50% | A | 30 | 60 min | No |
| I (MCQ) — 50% | B | 15 | 45 min | Yes |
| II (FRQ) — 50% | A | 2 | 30 min | Yes |
| II (FRQ) — 50% | B | 4 | 60 min | No |

Total: 45 MCQ + 6 FRQ, 3 hours 15 minutes
