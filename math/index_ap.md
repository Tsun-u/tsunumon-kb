# AP Calculus AB & BC Curriculum Reference

> Source: College Board AP Calculus AB and BC Course and Exam Description (CED)
> Three Big Ideas: **Change (CHA)**, **Limits (LIM)**, **Analysis of Functions (FUN)**

## Mathematical Practices
1. **Implementing Mathematical Processes** — Determine expressions and values using mathematical procedures and rules
2. **Connecting Representations** — Translate mathematical information across multiple representations
3. **Justification** — Justify reasoning and solutions
4. **Communication and Notation** — Use correct notation, language, and mathematical conventions

---

## AP Calculus AB — 8 Units

| Unit | Title | AB Exam Weight | Class Periods |
|------|-------|---------------|---------------|
| 1 | Limits and Continuity | 10–12% | ~22–23 |
| 2 | Differentiation: Definition and Fundamental Properties | 10–12% | ~13–14 |
| 3 | Differentiation: Composite, Implicit, and Inverse Functions | 9–13% | ~10–11 |
| 4 | Contextual Applications of Differentiation | 10–15% | ~10–11 |
| 5 | Analytical Applications of Differentiation | 15–18% | ~15–16 |
| 6 | Integration and Accumulation of Change | 17–20% | ~18–20 |
| 7 | Differential Equations | 6–12% | ~8–9 |
| 8 | Applications of Integration | 10–15% | ~12–13 |

## BC-Only Topics (not tested on AB exam)
- **6.11** Integration by Parts
- **6.12** Integration Using Linear Partial Fractions
- **6.13** Evaluating Improper Integrals
- **7.5** Euler's Method
- **7.9** Logistic Models with Differential Equations
- **8.13** Arc Length of a Smooth, Planar Curve and Distance Traveled
- **Unit 9** (all topics) — Parametric, Polar, Vector-Valued Functions
- **Unit 10** (all topics) — Infinite Sequences and Series

## Exam Format (AB and BC identical structure)

| Section | Part | Questions | Time | Calculator |
|---------|------|-----------|------|------------|
| I (MCQ) — 50% | A | 30 | 60 min | No |
| I (MCQ) — 50% | B | 15 | 45 min | Yes |
| II (FRQ) — 50% | A | 2 | 30 min | Yes |
| II (FRQ) — 50% | B | 4 | 60 min | No |

Total: 45 MCQ + 6 FRQ, 3 hours 15 minutes
